About Me

My name is Shawn and I love my family, coding, and making music. I have been married to my wife Kalli for nearly 12 years and we have 3 wonderful kids: Porter, Olivia, and Foss.


I began my coding adventure a year ago as I started to explore Java development. I felt like I was not synthesizing the information I was learning into anything cohesive, so I started a course in web development offered through the University of Utah. I have been able to learn at an accelerated rate with the help of amazing instructors and peers.


I have been making music for over 20 years and I am currently the senior tubist in the 23rd Army Band and perform regularly with the Utah Wind Symphony on the euphonium.
